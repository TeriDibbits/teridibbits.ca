Tim Hortons Brochure

Objective

The objective of this project was to build and design a brochure for a company following their brand.

Process

1. Research

I began by researching Tim Hortons, their history and how their brand evvolved over the years. They currently don’t have a brochure so I researched ads and the designs on their cups and other packaging.

2. Gathering Assets

Before I started to sketch I gathered high quality assets. This included images of their products, customer service shots, and their logo. I wanted to find simple images of their products without distracting backgrounds.

3. Brand

Tim Hortons colour palette is easily recognizable and very familiar to Canadian consumers. Most people can recognize their coffee cup from a distance. Their cup designs on may vary from year to year, so I chose a popular design that many will still recognize.
