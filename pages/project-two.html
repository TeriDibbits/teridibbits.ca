A&W Ad. Campaign

Objective: The objective was to create an ad campaign that creativity to capture the attention of the target audience through message and design. The client was A&W a fast food restaurant looking to further advertise their breakfast.

Concept: The concept is targeting people who eat eggs in the morning. A&W not only cracks your eggs they can make them anyway you like them. This offers so much more to the customer. People can be picky about their eggs. If they don’t like their eggs scrambled they can still order eggs but have them made sunny side up.

Photography: I chose imagery that wasn’t cooked because you see already made food everywhere. Breakfast sandwiches, eggs, bacon and toast already made. You don’t want your ad have to count on looking better then every other fast food restaurant offering breakfast. You need something different to grab people’s attention.
