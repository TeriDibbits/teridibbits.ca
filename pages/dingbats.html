Tackle Box Dingbat Design

Objective

The objective of this project was create simple dingbats with as little detail as possible, while still capturing the subjects appearance.

Process

1. Ideation

I decided I wanted to make dingbats for fishing. I then began to brainstorm ideas and broke down my ideas and categorized them. The direction I chose was fishing lures because of the variety of fun shapes. I knew this was a subject that could be simplified.

2. Research

Before I could being sketching I had to research the different kinds of lures and the important shapes that identify them. After decided on a variety of lures I started sketching the basic shape, identifying important features of each.

3. Tackle Box

Each lure design has a unique feature that helps identity it and its individual purpose. The clean approach to simplify the designs of the lures captures the basic shape of the various lures fisherman use on a daily basis.
